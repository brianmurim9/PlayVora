Blessed Home Help Center

Blessed Home Help Center is a children's home dedicated to giving vulnerable children a safe and hopeful future. We support orphans and street children by providing shelter, nutritious meals, education, healthcare, and emotional care. Our mission is to nurture every child with love, guidance, and opportunity so they can grow into confident, independent individuals and build a better life.
